What we offer

One ecosystem. Four connected pillars.

ITM Green Mobility is not a single taxi company — it is a connected electric mobility operating system where every pillar feeds the next: rides, corporate contracts, charging energy and fleet operations.

Green Cab · Ride

Green Ride

An electric rider & driver marketplace — as simple as Uber, as driver-fair as Bolt.

  • Instant booking & upfront fares
  • Live driver matching & tracking
  • Digital payment & ratings
  • Ride safety & SOS
Green Cab · Business

Green Business

Corporate mobility for companies, embassies, hotels and institutions — on contract.

  • Corporate accounts & policies
  • Dedicated drivers & vehicles
  • Monthly invoicing & spend controls
  • Sustainability & carbon reports
Green Cab · Charge

Green Charge

A standalone EV charging network and station-management business.

  • Charger discovery & availability
  • kWh, revenue & uptime tracking
  • Queue & fault management
  • Driver charging wallet
Green Cab · Fleet

Green Fleet

EV vehicle & driver operations, including lease-to-own models.

  • Vehicle & driver onboarding
  • Battery health & maintenance
  • Lease-to-own deductions
  • Profitability per vehicle

How the ecosystem flows

Energy in. Movement out. Value all the way through.

Every ride runs on the same corridor: a rider requests, a battery-aware driver is matched, the trip completes, payment settles, and the vehicle recharges — feeding fleet and charging revenue back into the system.

1

Request

Rider books & sees an upfront fare

2

Match

Battery-aware dispatch assigns a driver

3

Ride

Live tracking, safety & clean trip

4

Pay

Mobile money, card, wallet or corporate

5

Recharge

Vehicle recharges — revenue loops back
