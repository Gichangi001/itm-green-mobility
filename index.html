Our mission

Make clean movement the easy choice in Benin.

ITM Green Mobility exists so that getting around Cotonou — to work, to the airport, to a hospital, to a client meeting — never has to cost the air we breathe. We build the rides, the charging network and the fleet tools that make electric the obvious, affordable option, not the sacrifice.

Clean by default

Every vehicle on the platform is 100% electric — zero tailpipe emissions on every single trip, from the first ride to the last.

Income for Beninese drivers

Lease-to-own vehicles, transparent fares and instant cash-out — built to create real, sustainable income for women, youth and independent drivers.

Built for Benin, built to scale

Designed around Cotonou's roads, payment habits and climate first — with the same playbook ready for West Africa's next cities.

How the ecosystem flows

Energy in. Movement out. Value all the way through.

Every ride runs on the same corridor: a rider requests, a battery-aware driver is matched, the trip completes, payment settles, and the vehicle recharges — feeding fleet and charging revenue back into the system.
